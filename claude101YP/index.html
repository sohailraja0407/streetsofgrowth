The Anthropic dimension

This flywheel has an external loop that Jordan's LinkedIn journey does not: Anthropic itself. Every time Marcus posts about Claude 101 and tags @Anthropic, he creates a signal Anthropic's comms and policy teams can act on. This is a deliberate and documentable public narrative that SoG controls.

Marcus's posting plan — AI-led weighting, LinkedIn secondary

Weighting note: Marcus's primary audience is the AI/tech sector, youth employment advocates, and Anthropic directly. Posts marked AI lead are the priority. Posts marked LinkedIn serve the broader professional network.

Getting Anthropic to notice — and ideally respond

Why this is a credible ask
  • SoG is a registered charity working with 15-21 year olds facing genuine social exclusion
  • Marcus co-designed the curriculum: this is youth-led, not top-down
  • The certificate is concrete and verifiable — Anthropic can point to it
  • SoG's use case aligns with Anthropic's stated mission around AI that is safe and beneficial to all
  • The programme is scalable nationally
What we are actually asking for
  • A public acknowledgement — comment, repost, or mention — that legitimises the certificate
  • Ideally: Anthropic co-brands the Claude 101 certificate so it carries weight on a CV
  • Optionally: SoG included as a case study in Anthropic's responsible AI access reporting
  • Not asking for funding initially — asking for recognition, which costs nothing

Outreach sequence

Step 1Marcus posts his certificate on LinkedIn
Tags @Anthropic directly. Frames it as "the first young person from a Streets of Growth cohort to complete Claude 101." Personal and specific, not promotional. SoG staff engage within the hour.
Step 2SoG reshares with organisational framing
SoG's page shares Marcus's post with a statement about the co-designed programme. Tags Anthropic's company page. Links to any published write-up.
Step 3Direct outreach to Anthropic comms
Sohail emails Anthropic's communications team directly. Subject: "Young person co-designs AI literacy programme with SoG — certificate issued." Attaches post link, one-page summary, and certificate.
Step 4Sector press creates the external signal
Third Sector or Civil Society News runs a short piece. Marcus's story is the hook; programme scalability is the angle. Moves Anthropic from optional engagement to active response.
